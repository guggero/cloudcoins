Service

Cloudcoins is intended as a secure, zero-knowledge key store for your Crypto Currency private and public keys.

Zero-knowledge

Your password encrypts everything and never leaves your browser. It is never transmitted to our servers and is not stored in your browser either. All your keys are encrypted with your password before storing them in our database.

Open Source

You don't need to blindly trust us. Have a look at the source code to see if the application really does what we promise.

All Altcoins

Our goal is to support every Altcoin/Crypto Currency there is and allow you to create and manage keys for all of them. Of course that's a lot of work, so we always welcome contributions!

Convenient

We want to make Crypto Currencies as easy to use as possible. So instead of a hardware wallet we offer something similar as a web service.
